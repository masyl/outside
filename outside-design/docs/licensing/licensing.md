### Why MIT License?

**Our Rationale**: To make the platform as inclusive and useful as possible, it's better to make it free as in free beer and free as in freedom to build what you want with it. Something else will have to be figured out for monetization.

The MIT License was chosen because it:

- **Maximizes Freedom**: Allows anyone to use, modify, and distribute the code for any purpose
- **Is Commercial-Friendly**: Permits use in proprietary software without copyleft restrictions
- **Is Simple and Clear**: Short, easy-to-understand text that minimizes legal ambiguity
- **Has Wide Adoption**: One of the most popular open-source licenses with strong community support
